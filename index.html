About Me

I Am
Still
Learning

I live in the Fort Lincoln neighborhood of Northeast Washington, DC with my wife, two kids, and a fish named Orchid. As a husband and father, I endeavor to grow each day in love. As a research analyst, I aim to grow in knowledge. And as an engaged citizen, I strive to grow in efficacy.

I hope to leave my neighborhood and my city a better, more just and equitably place, not just for the sake of my own children, but for everyone who calls these places home.
