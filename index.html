Ward 5 EEC

The Ward 5 Education Equity Committee (W5EEC) was established in 2019 by Representative Zachary Parker (Ward 5 member on the DC State Board of Education) to give Ward 5 neighbors voice on DC education policy matters. If you would like to join in this work, please reach out to me (Robert Henderson) at hendersr21 at gmail dot com.
