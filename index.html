I live in the Fort Lincoln neighborhood of Northeast Washington, DC with my wife, two kids, and a fish named Orchid. As a husband and father, I endeavor to grow in love. As a research analyst, I aim to grow in knowledge. And as an engaged citizen, I strive to grow in efficacy.
